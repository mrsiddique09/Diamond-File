My favorite sports

Cricket

Cricket is a globally popular sport played between two teams of eleven players each. The game revolves around batting, bowling, and fielding, with the objective of scoring more runs than the opposing team. Matches can range from short Twenty20 formats to longer Test matches that span five days. Originating in England, cricket has become a cultural phenomenon in countries like India, Australia, and Pakistan. It’s known for its strategic depth, thrilling moments, and passionate fan base.

Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a fast-paced team sport that originated in ancient India and is now popular across South Asia. It involves two teams taking turns to send a "raider" into the opponent's half to tag players and return without being tackled. The raider must chant "kabaddi" continuously while in enemy territory, adding a unique vocal challenge to the game. Strength, strategy, and agility are key to both attacking and defending. Kabaddi has gained international recognition, with leagues and tournaments held worldwide.

Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is the world's most popular sport played by two teams of eleven players. The objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net using any part of the body except the hands and arms. Matches are played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end, and each game lasts 90 minutes, split into two halves. Football is celebrated for its fast pace, teamwork, and thrilling moments that unite fans across the globe. Major tournaments like the FIFA World Cup draw billions of viewers and showcase the sport's global appeal.
